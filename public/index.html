The plan

A practical operating system for trusted metered work.

Our roadmap is to make Synaptic Metering the place where a customer can sign in, start a monitored work session, watch account intelligence accumulate, and leave with invoice evidence that explains what happened, when it happened, and which authenticated account owns it.

01 • Access

Authenticated account portal

Google sign-in and passkeys protect the console so usage, invoice history, and intelligence strings stay scoped to the right account.

02 • Metering

Session heartbeat ledger

Apps and operators create sessions, send heartbeats, and convert elapsed activity into billable seconds with durable summaries.

03 • Evidence

Invoices customers can audit

Invoice exports are backed by normalized records, account authorization, and verification endpoints rather than static PDF-only billing.

How it will work

From live activity to a trusted billing artifact.

Start

A customer or integrated service opens an authenticated metering session from the console or API.

Observe

Heartbeat events record ongoing work and can be enriched by Genesis stream evidence, entropy settings, and anchored map context.

Summarize

The backend calculates elapsed billable seconds, merges account intelligence, and prepares a session summary.

Verify

Invoice verification checks ownership and normalized payload integrity before the record is shared or imported.

Operate

Administrators monitor health, map anchors, and catalog pricing while customers keep a transparent history of billed work.
